Product & Sizing

How do I choose the right size?

CURREX insoles are designed to be trim-to-fit. Simply select your US shoe size range, then trim the insole along the marked guidelines to match your exact shoe size. Refer to our Fitting at Home Guide for detailed instructions.

What shoe sizes do CURREX insoles fit?

CURREX insoles are available for US Men's sizes 4-16 and Women's sizes 5-17. Each pair covers a range of sizes and can be trimmed to fit your specific shoe size.

What is Dynamic Arch Technology?

Dynamic Arch Technology (DAT) is CURREX's patented arch support system. Unlike traditional insoles that have a fixed arch height, DAT features interchangeable arch inserts that allow you to customize the arch support to your foot type - whether you have a high arch, medium arch, low arch, or flat arch.

How do I determine my arch type?

You can determine your arch type at home using the Wet Test: wet your foot and step on a piece of paper or dark surface. The shape of your footprint will indicate your arch type. For a detailed guide, visit our Fitting at Home page.

Can CURREX insoles be transferred between shoes?

Yes! CURREX insoles can be easily transferred between shoes as long as they fit the new shoes properly. Simply remove them from one pair and place them in another.

How do I trim my insoles?

Place the insole on top of your shoe's existing insole or stand on the insole to mark where your heel aligns. Use sharp scissors to trim along the guidelines printed on the bottom of the insole. Trim small amounts at a time for the best fit.

Sports & Activities

Which insole is best for running?

The RUNPRO is specifically designed for running. It provides the perfect balance of cushioning and support for runners seeking comfort and efficiency.

Which insole is best for hiking?

The HIKEPRO is engineered for trail running and hiking. It offers enhanced stability for uneven terrain and provides excellent support during long days on the trail.

Which insole is best for soccer or football?

The CLEATPRO is designed for field sports including soccer, football, and baseball. Its low-volume fit and lateral support make it ideal for cleated sports.

Which insole is best for everyday use?

The SUPPORTSTP is designed for everyday walking, standing, and light activity. It provides all-day comfort and support for daily use.

Which insole is best for basketball?

The BASKETBALLPRO is optimized for jumping, cutting, and court movement. It provides the support and cushioning needed for the dynamic movements of basketball.

Care & Maintenance

How long do CURREX insoles last?

With regular use, CURREX insoles typically last 6-12 months depending on the intensity of use and the type of activity. Replace them when you notice a significant decrease in support or cushioning.

How do I clean my insoles?

Spot clean your insoles with a mild soap and water solution. Allow them to air dry completely before placing them back in your shoes. Do not machine wash or dry.

Can I use CURREX insoles in any shoe?

CURREX insoles work best in shoes with removable insoles. They are compatible with most athletic shoes, running shoes, hiking boots, casual sneakers, and work boots.
